Great Uni Hack 2025 - University of Manchester

In November 2025, I partook in a Hackathon in Manchester. This hackathon was organised by the UniCs society.


During this hackathon, we completed several challenges from Hackchester. Thanks to my excellent team, we were able to win the cybersecurity competition. It consisted of a four-part CTF challenge where we had to analyze digital forensics, perform reverse engineering, interact with a Flask web app using cURL, and break out of a PyJail by exploiting an audit-hook vulnerability to read a text file.

I would like to thank my team for their strong support.
